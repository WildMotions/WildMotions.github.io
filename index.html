Who am I.

Hi, my name is Nelson Rodrigues, and this is WildMotions. Every motion has a story and how you tell it, makes all the difference. The founder, graduated from University of Aveiro in Multimedia, awakend the passion for audiovisual content. His love for the cinematic world makes it possible to do creative works exploring every angle. The scope is to create inovative work, not just simple capturing straightforward images or designing "ok" illustrations, but to share his love for things. The sense of trying to achieve perfection gives him the ability to dedicate himself to the fullest with the project at hand, knowing every client is unique. In Nature, things can be complex. And versatility can overcome the greatests barriers.
With this in mind, borns WildMotions. WildMotions is all about showing the hidden details in life in a speacial way. The motto of this project is to show you everyting is possible, and that joy of every moment should be captured in its essense.

"Motion Is the sign of life"
